Nihari Recipe

Nihari is a popular Pakistani stew made with slow-cooked meat, typically beef or mutton, along with spices. It's a rich and flavorful dish, often enjoyed with naan or rice.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg beef or mutton
  • 2 onions, sliced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 inches ginger, grated
  • 1/2 cup wheat flour
  • 2 tablespoons ghee or oil
  • 2 tablespoons Nihari masala
  • Salt to taste
  • Water, as needed

Method

  1. Heat ghee or oil in a large pot over medium heat.
  2. Add sliced onions and cook until golden brown.
  3. Add minced garlic and grated ginger, saut√© for 2 minutes.
  4. Add meat and cook until it changes color.
  5. Add Nihari masala and salt, mix well.
  6. Add enough water to cover the meat and bring to a boil.
  7. Reduce heat, cover, and simmer for 2-3 hours until the meat is tender.
  8. In a separate pan, dry roast wheat flour until golden brown.
  9. Add the roasted wheat flour to the meat mixture and stir well to thicken.
  10. Simmer for another 10-15 minutes until the desired consistency is reached.
  11. Serve hot with naan or rice.
