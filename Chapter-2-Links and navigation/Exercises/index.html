Plot Overview

GTA V is an action-adventure game set in the fictional state of San Andreas, based on Southern California. The game follows the story of three criminals as they plan and execute a series of heists to secure their own futures. Their paths intersect and conflict with each other as they navigate through a world of crime, corruption, and betrayal.

Characters

Michael De Santa

A retired bank robber living under witness protection.

Franklin Clinton

A young and ambitious street hustler looking for better opportunities.

Trevor Philips

A volatile and psychopathic career criminal.
